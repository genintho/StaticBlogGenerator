Ingredients

  • X Duck legs
  • N Carotts
  • M Potatoes
  • Green olives
  • Garlic
  • Thyms
  • Feuilles de lauriers

Let's do it!

Remove some greases of your Duck legs if they are really greasy.

Roast the legs, on the skins in a pane. You don't need to add grease. After a few minutes turn the leg on there other face. Wait few more minutes and remove them from the fire.

In the oven

Transfer the legs from the pane to a oven friendly plate. Put them in the oven, at 350F during around 45min. During this time keep an eye on the legs, add some water on them.

After the 40min, remove the plate from the oven, and remove the legs from it. Press the garlics, some green olives in small pieces, add the potatoes and carots cut in small pieces.

In the oven

Add salt, black pepper, thyms. Put that back to the oven for an other 45 min.

That's it! Enjoy.
